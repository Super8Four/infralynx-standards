# Licensing Decision

## Decision

InfraLynx will begin as a private commercial project.

## Rationale

- The platform is intended for enterprise delivery.
- Product and licensing boundaries are expected to evolve during early architecture work.
- Private governance allows standards, workflows, and architecture to stabilize before any community release model is considered.

## Future Option

- Apache 2.0 may be considered for a future community edition.
- Enterprise-specific capabilities, support terms, and licensing may remain separate.
